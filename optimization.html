Aquascaping conditions optimization

Livestock

  • Avoid too high bioload
  • Use algae eaters like red cherries and Clithon sp.

Water Parameters

  • KH <9 or with stem plants even lower
  • Keep KH Swings during WC within 3

Flow

  • Enough but don't exaggerate (does the co2 mist come back towards outflow?)
  • Don't fully fill filters with media
  • Easy plants in dead spots

Co2

  • Ph drop 1,2 during photoperiod and keep it stable
  • Fine mist
  • Gas exchange (proper surface ripple and mixing with deeper layers)

Nutrients

  • NPK NO3 not too low (<5) or high (>10) depending on plant mass and if plants are in aqua soil.
  • Micros (proper fe chelates)
  • Magnesium
  • Root tabs

Light

  • Can the plants handle shadows or high light?
  • Proper spectrum for color display

Maintenance

  • Prevent overcrowded plants
  • Trim old and unhealthy plants
  • Clean filters, hoses,inflow, co2 membrane and purigen regularly
  • One or twee weekly water changes with turkey baster cleaning
  • Give plants time to adapt to new conditions
